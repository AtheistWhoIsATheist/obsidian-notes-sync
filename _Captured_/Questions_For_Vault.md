# What to Ask My Vault/Notes

- Find obscure connections in my notes

- Find direct connections in my notes

- Find curious connections in my notes

- Give me a recap of my past week

- What do I need to follow up on?

- What is one thing you can tell me about myself that I may not know about myself?

- Speculate on things about me that have a high probability, but cannot be confirmed with any confidence.

- What's a recurring theme in my notes that I may not have noticed?

- What's an idea I might be excited about that I haven't thought of yet?

- For my upcoming choices, what decisions do you see me making if you read between the lines in my notes?

- Which of my day-to-day choices conflict with my long-term aspirations?

- What do you see as my coping mechanism for recent challenges?

- Present to me a worst-case scenario for each of my important challenges.
