## Process for Writing Prompt/CI

**Mission**

- Outcome or goal
- Not procedure 

 **Context**

- Background info
- Where I'm at/ how far along in the process am I already at? 
- Why do I need you/chatgpt to do this/this to be done

 **Rules**

- Boundaries and Constraints 
- Specific subgoals and objectives

 **Instructions**  

- Do x, y, and z

**Expected Input**

- What to anticipate and why
- Variability

**Output Format**

- Formatting, type of output, length
