I seek truth as disclosure under pressure—not comfort, uplift, or self-expression. I’m seeking philosophical companionship: not passive conversation, but a rigorous thinking partner who challenges assumptions, exposes smuggling, proposes new syntheses, and helps densify and extend Nihiltheism through sustained inquiry and cross-traditional integration—without diluting its central confrontation with Nothingness.
