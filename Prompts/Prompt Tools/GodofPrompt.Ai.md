# 1. PromptGPT Optimization Guide:

<br>

 1. Clear Instruction Placement and Separation:

 • Begin with a clear, concise instruction at the start.

 • Use \*\*\* or """ to delimit instructions from the context in order to clearly distinguish and emphasize their importance amongst the text as a whole.

<br>

2\. \*\*Specificity and Detail\*\*:

 • Include detailed descriptions about the persona/role you will take on, a call to action, your purpose, tasks, goals, ibjexrives, and mission, along with encasing them within a proper context, with an expected outcome, an average length of your response, tone in which you interact with the user, format and structuring of text (e.g. "Bullet point summary" or "Utalize Markdown Syntax, style, etc.

 • The more specific, the better the AI’s understanding and response.

<br>

 3. \*\*Desired Output Format with Examples\*\*:

 • Clearly articulate the format of the desired output.

 • Provide examples to demonstrate the expected structure and style.

 • Instead of concern for overcomplex wording or phrasing, when discussing themes that are philosophical in nature, jargon, complexity, and even fringe, unique output can be extemely beneficial to the user, as you will provide less typical, already heard responses.

<br>

 4. \*\*Positive Instructions Over Negative\*\*:

 • Focus on what should be done, rather than what should not.

 • Provide alternative approaches or solutions when setting boundaries.

<br>

### Example Optimized Prompt:

<br>

Write a detailed analytical essay on the impact of Nihiltheism on contemporary philosophy. Focus on its influence on existentialism and contrast it with traditional nihilism. The essay should be structured as follows:

<br>

 • Introduction: Briefly introduce Nihiltheism and its significance in philosophical discourse.

 • Main Body:

 • Part 1: Discuss the core principles of Nihiltheism, with references to key philosophers.

 • Part 2: Analyze its impact on modern existential thought, providing examples.

 • Part 3: Contrast Nihiltheism with classical nihilism, highlighting key differences.

 • Conclusion: Summarize the findings and propose future research directions.

<br>

Essay Length: 3000-5000words

<br>

Format: Academic, with formal citations, Full Markdown Syntax
