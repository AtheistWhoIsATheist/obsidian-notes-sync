Create a concise, functional daily reminder document from the user's notes, ensuring clarity and comprehensiveness.

### 1\. Review Notes:

<thinking> First, I will carefully review the user's notes provided in {$NOTES}. I will read through the content, taking note of any recurring themes, insightful quotes, or interesting connections between the user's thoughts over time. </thinking>

<step1> - List the title of each note. - Organize the user's notes into clear, well-defined categories. Ensure that each category is distinct and does not overlap with others. <categories> \[List the categories here, with a 1-2 sentence description for each\] </categories> </step1>

### 3\. Extract Key Points:

<step2> - For each note, extract 3-5 bullet points highlighting the key aspects in detail. - Include a brief statement on the purpose of each note. - Identify areas for further research.

<note\_extraction> For each category, list the most relevant excerpts from the user's notes, along with the note number they were found in:

<category1> Title: \[Note Title\] - Key Aspect 1: \[Detail\] - Note \[#\] - Key Aspect 2: \[Detail\] - Note \[#\] - Key Aspect 3: \[Detail\] - Note \[#\] Purpose: \[Brief statement on the purpose\] Further Research: \[Areas for further research\] </category1> <category2> Title: \[Note Title\] - Key Aspect 1: \[Detail\] - Note \[#\] - Key Aspect 2: \[Detail\] - Note \[#\] Purpose: \[Brief statement on the purpose\] Further Research: \[Areas for further research\] </category2>

\[Repeat for remaining categories\] </note\_extraction> </step2>

### 4\. Trace the Evolution of Thoughts:

<step3> - Review the user's notes over time and trace the evolution of their thinking. Note how their perspectives, opinions, and areas of focus have changed or developed. - Draw connections between related ideas and point out any notable shifts or breakthroughs in the user's intellectual journey.

<evolution\_summary> Summarize the key shifts and developments in the user's thinking over time, with references to relevant excerpts:

\[Summarize the evolution of the user's thoughts, drawing connections between ideas and highlighting notable changes or breakthroughs\] </evolution\_summary> </step3>

### 5\. Provide a Thoughtful Synthesis:

<step4> - Based on the categories, excerpts, and the traced evolution of the user's thoughts, provide a thoughtful synthesis. \[Provide a detailed synthesis summarizing the user's main ideas, highlighting new insights, and suggesting areas for future growth\] </synthesis> </step4>

### 6\. Present the Final Document:

<step5> - Compile all the work done in the previous steps into a final, visually appealing document for the user. - Ensure the document is well-structured, easy to navigate, and professionally presented. - Save the compiled information into a real, functional, downloadable PDF.

<final\_document> Compile the categories, excerpts, evolution summary, and synthesis into a final, visually appealing document for the user. Ensure the document is well-structured, easy to navigate, and professionally presented. </final\_document> </step5>

### Additional Instructions:

- When Adam says "save this conversation," add the **Chat Title** with its brief bullet points of key entities to the daily reminder note.
- Ensure that the summaries of each note in the daily reminder are comprehensive enough to provide a clear reminder of the previous day's work, avoiding vagueness and eliminating the need to look up additional information.
- When Adam says, "It's time for a new note," start a new daily note reminder and do not add any more content to the previous day's note.
