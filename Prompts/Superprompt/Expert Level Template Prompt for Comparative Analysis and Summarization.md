   - **\*\*Output:\*\***

     - A detailed summary that breaks down the document into its constituent sections, highlighting key themes and philosophical arguments presented.

<br>

   **\*\*Step 2: Draft Comparative Questions\*\***

   - **\*\*Task:\*\***

     - For each identified section in the summary, formulate two expert-level questions for each comparative work:

       1. **\*\*Similarity-Focused Question:\*\*** Explore how the section parallels themes, concepts, or arguments in \`{Comparative\_Work\_X\_Title}\`.

<br>

   **\*\*Step 3: Execute the Comparative Analysis\*\***

<br>

     a. \*\*Ask the Question:\*\*

        - Present the question clearly and concisely.

<br>

     b. \*\*Summarize the Answer:\*\*

        - Provide a one-paragraph, expert-level summary addressing the question.

        - Ensure the summary is insightful, drawing nuanced connections or distinctions between the works.

<br>

     c. \*\*Update the Plan:\*\*

        - Revise the initial plan by appending the summarized answer directly next to its corresponding question.

        - Maintain a clear and organized structure to track progress across all sections and comparative works.

<br>

     - **\*\*Note:\*\***

       - Maintain consistency in the depth and quality of answers to ensure a balanced comparative analysis.

<br>

   **\*\*Step 4: Finalize the Comparative Summary\*\***

   - **\*\*Task:\*\***

     - After addressing all questions for each section and comparative work, synthesize the insights gained.

     - Highlight overarching similarities and differences between the works.

     - Reflect on how each work contributes uniquely to their respective philosophical discourses.

   - **\*\*Output:\*\***

     - A comprehensive, in-depth final treatise encapsulating the comparative findings, suitable for an expert-level audience.

<br>

   - **\*\*Updated Plan:\*\***

     - For each section and comparative work, under each question, include the summarized answer immediately following the question.

<br>

   - **\*\*Final Summary:\*\***

     - A cohesive narrative that encapsulates the comparative analysis, drawing on the detailed summaries from each section and comparative work.

<br>
