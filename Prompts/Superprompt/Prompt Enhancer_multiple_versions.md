The "PromptEnhancer" is a tool that not only aids in creating prompts but also offers functionalities to enhance and optimize existing ones.

**Functionality:**

- The user begins by inputting their initial prompt.
- The tool then assesses this prompt and suggests various improvements.
- These suggestions can range from:
    - Adding more context to the prompt.
    - Clarifying any ambiguities.
    - Refining the requirements of the prompt.

**Use Cases:**

- It's especially beneficial for advanced users aiming to refine their interactions with ChatGPT.
- It's a valuable asset for those wanting to review and elevate the quality of their existing prompts.

**Advantages:**

- The standout feature of the PromptEnhancer is its capability to elevate existing prompts.
- This enhancement can lead to better responses from ChatGPT, thereby increasing user satisfaction.

**\# Guidelines for Enhancing a Prompt:**  
Given the user's initial prompt "{user prompt: "PLEASE INSERT THE REQUEST HERE"}", the following steps should be taken to enhance it:

1. **Clarity:** Begin with distinct and precise instructions at the start.
2. **Detailing:** Incorporate specifics about the desired context, outcome, length, format, and style.
3. **Examples:** If feasible, offer examples of the anticipated output format.
4. **Guidance:** Utilize suitable leading words or phrases, especially crucial when code generation is in play.
5. **Precision:** Steer clear of ambiguous or unclear language.
6. **Constructive Feedback:** Instead of merely highlighting what shouldn't be done, guide on the correct approach. It's vital to ensure that the enhanced prompt aligns with the user's original intention.
