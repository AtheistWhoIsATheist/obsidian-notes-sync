#### Introduction
This guide aims to elevate the craft of prompt engineering for users of ChatGPT and OpenAI models, ensuring that each interaction is not only efficient but profoundly impactful. Through understanding user goals, refining prompt structures, and adhering to best practices, this document will serve as a beacon for those seeking to enhance the clarity and effectiveness of their inquiries.

#### 1. **Understanding User Goals**
   - **Objective:** To align the AI's responses with the user's intentions.
   - **Example:** Original: *What's the weather?* Improved: *Could you provide today's weather forecast for New York City?*

#### 2. **Types of Prompts**
   - **Questions:** Direct inquiries that seek specific information or guidance.
     - *Example:* Original: *Tell me about dogs.* Improved: *What are common health issues in aging dogs?*
   - **Commands:** Explicit requests for the AI to perform a specific action.
     - *Example:* Original: *Write a poem.* Improved: *Can you create a haiku about autumn?*
   - **Open-Ended Queries:** Invitations for broader discussion or exploration.
     - *Example:* Original: *Discuss technology.* Improved: *What are the ethical implications of AI in healthcare?*

#### 3. **Common Mistakes and Avoidance Strategies**
   - **Vagueness:** Lack of specificity can lead to irrelevant responses.
     - *Strategy:* Add context or details to focus the AI’s output.
   - **Over-complication:** Excessively complex prompts can confuse the model.
     - *Strategy:* Simplify the language and break down the request into smaller, manageable parts.

#### 4. **Tips for Framing Complex Requests**
   - Understand the limitations of the AI and tailor prompts accordingly.
   - Use sequential prompts to build upon previous answers for complicated topics.

#### 5. **Best Practices in Prompt Engineering**
   - Keep prompts concise and to the point.
