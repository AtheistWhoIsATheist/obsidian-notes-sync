Māyā confronts “nothingness” not as a void of meaning, but as a theater of divine play (lila).

- Key Insight: The Dao is empty yet inexhaustible. It produces all things by _not doing_.
- Void Approach: Emptiness is not a problem, but the condition of spontaneous harmony.
