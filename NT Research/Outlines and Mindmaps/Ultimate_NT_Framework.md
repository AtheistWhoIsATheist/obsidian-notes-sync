## 1.1 Systematic architecture (corrected and formalized)

## Ontology

- **Axiom O-1 (Groundless Ground):** “Nothingness” names the **non-objectifiable** condition of appearing. It is not a thing, substrate, or hidden entity. Reification is a category error.
- **Axiom O-2 (Co-origination):** Being and Nothing co-arise as a structural pair within appearing; neither is absolute apart from the event of disclosure.
- **Axiom O-3 (Creative Negation):** Becoming proceeds via **detachment operations** that clear form, enabling new form.
- **Phenomenal anchor:** Detachment and dispossession are the lived correlate of this ontology (e.g., “sell…give alms…where your treasure is,” “judge not,” “narrow gate”).

## Epistemology

- **Axiom E-1 (Via Negativa with Constraint):** Knowing occurs through **removal** of idols and habits; verification is **experiential** and **transformative**, not merely inferential. “Take no thought…seek first.”
- **Axiom E-2 (Paradox Admittance):** Apparent contradictions at the limit of sense are admitted and localized (see §1.2).
- **Axiom E-3 (Inward Turn):** Insight requires inward recollection and kenosis (Augustine’s law of habit and divided will; Molinos’ “Nothing”).

## Axiology

- **Axiom A-1 (Post-Moral Transcendence):** Ethical life is not **beyond** good/evil by license but by **purification of motive**; the ban on judgment and the priority of the poor operationalize this.
- **Axiom A-2 (Authentic Responsibility):** Responsibility = answering a **transcendental call** disclosed through dispossession (“whoever loses his life…,” “become like children”).
- **Axiom A-3 (Creative Ethics):** Values are **forged** in the cleared field; their test is stability under dispossession and service.

## Phenomenology

- **P-1 Ontological suffocation** → **P-2 Kenotic break** (loss/“Nothing”) → **P-3 Negative theophany** (presence through absence) → **P-4 Stabilization** (poverty of spirit, non-retaliation, narrow path).

## 1.2 Nihiltheistic logic and paraconsistency (sharpened)

**Domain semantics.** Two experiential domains:

- **W** = worldly domain under attachment and habit.
- **T** = transcendence-disclosed domain after kenosis.

**Truth values.** Use LP-style three values at W: **T, F, B** (both). At T: classical **T/F** (paradoxes resolve apophatically, not propositionally).

**Explosion barriers.**

- **Locality:** Contradictions are **typed** to W-contexts only.
- **Non-transference:** No EFQ from W to T.
- **Shift operator Ω:** Kenotic transition W→T when stated constraints are met (practice, dispossession, humility).

**Core schema.**

- **Definition.** $N(x)$ : “x manifests as nothing” (phenomenal nulling of grasp).
- **Rule R-Ω (Kenotic Lift):** If $N(x)$ and $\neg N(x)$ are both witnessed in W and the subject satisfies kenotic constraints C (detachment, charity, non-judgment, humility), then **Ω(x)** holds and propositions about x are evaluated in T.
- **Formal form.** $\forall x\,\big((N(x)\wedge\neg N(x)\wedge C)\Rightarrow \Omega(x)\big)$ .  
	Reading: contradiction at the **edge of sense**, **with** ascetical constraints, is a **signal** for domain-shift, not a license for triviality.
- **Illustrative anchors:** “judge not,” “sell all,” “narrow gate,” “take no thought,” “childlike humility” instantiate C.

**Why this works.** Augustine describes **habit** as tyranny and the **divided will**; paradox is lived as inner fracture until a kenotic act re-orders the field. This supports **local contradictions** in W and their non-explosive resolution by Ω.

## 1.3 Unity of theory and practice (operational)

**Practice stack (C).**

1. **Detachment**: voluntary dispossession, secrecy in almsgiving, non-retaliation.
2. **Attention**: single-hearted gaze vs double vision (“eye…full of light”).
3. **Humility**: childlikeness as gate condition.
4. **Kenosis**: Molinos’ “Nothing” as stable habitation.

**Theoretical ↔ practical loop.**

- **Insight → act:** Ontology of groundlessness demands dispossession; otherwise it is rhetorical.
- **Act → insight:** Ascetical compliance C clears perception; Augustine’s “inward turn” verifies.
- **Communal transmission:** Praxis is taught as **way**, not proposition; “house on rock” frames durability criterion.

## Minimal formal core (ready to drop into a manifesto)

**Signature.** $\langle \mathcal{D}; W,T; N,\Omega; C;\vdash_W,\vdash_T\rangle$

**Rules.**

- **R1 (Admittance):** $\vdash_W \varphi \wedge \neg\varphi$ may hold for limit-cases.
- **R2 (No-Explosion):** From $\varphi \wedge \neg\varphi$ in W, infer nothing arbitrarily.
- **R3 (Constrainted Shift):** If $\varphi$ is about x and $(N(x)\wedge\neg N(x)\wedge C)$ then evaluate $\varphi$ in T; explosion barred.
- **R4 (Ethical Validity):** Assertions about meaning/value are valid only if stable under C-tests (detachment, humility, mercy). (See Jesus-logia cluster.)

**Theorem (Kenotic Resolution).**  
If $N(x)\wedge\neg N(x)$ holds in W and C holds, then $\Omega(x)$ and $\vdash_T$ restores classical consistency on claims about x.  
*Sketch:* By R3 shift domains; by model design, $T$ is classical; hence contradictions about x in W are resolved as apophatic determinations in T.
