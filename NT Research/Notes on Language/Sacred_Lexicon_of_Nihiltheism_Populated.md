## Foundational Terms (NX0001–NX0091)

### NX0001 · Apophatic Refusal
A disciplined negation of any positive claim about ultimate reality. Refusal as sacred act. Not absence of belief, but an existential gesture of annihilating idolatry. To refuse is to revere what cannot be spoken.

### NX0002 · Recursive Pathway
A loop that deepens with each turn. Used within Nihiltheism to fracture epistemic certainty. Repetition becomes revelation. Meaning decays until something stranger than meaning appears.

### NX0003 · Ontological Negation
Negation not merely as denial, but as a metaphysical method: to affirm what is *only* through what it is not. Echoes in apophasis, deconstruction, and negative theology.

### NX0004 · Theological loo Collapse
The breakdown of inherited religious structures—not as a failure, but as a rite. Collapse is the passage into what remains after God has been unsaid.

### NX0005 · Cognitive Fracture
The splitting of conceptual coherence through recursive paradox. Fracture is not fragmentation—it is the condition of sacred receptivity. It renders thought porous to the Void.
