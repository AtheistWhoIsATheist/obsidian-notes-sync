#### 2.2. Transcendence Stress-Test (NT-Criterion)

The **NT-Criterion** is: _“Can this survive Infinite Presence through Nothingness without degenerating into comforting meaning fabrication?”_

|Conceptual Node|NT-Criterion Status|Justification/Apophatic Purification|
|:--|:--|:--|
|**Existentialism (Sartre/Camus)**|**FAIL (Partial)**|Fails by attempting to **create subjective meaning** in the void ("draw from it his strength"). This is a **meaning fabrication** that avoids the full implications of Nothingness.|
|**Traditional Theodicies**|**FAIL**|Fails by offering **"answers"** and **"rational defenses"** that are "hubris filled" and diminish the Other into human "oughts." They are **consolatory metaphysics**.|
|**Tillich's "Courage to Be"**|**FAIL (Borderline)**|Fails if interpreted as a **worldly courage** or an optimistic facade. It only **PASSES** if the "acceptance of despair is in itself faith," reducing the meaning of life to **"despair about the meaning of life."**|

|**Mystical Ego Dissolution**|**PASS (Augmented)**|Passes because it confronts the **Infinite Presence** by reducing the self to **nothing** ("myself without me"). It is a **blessed engulfment** that is consistent with the Nothingness.|
|**The Pursuit of Happiness**|**FAIL**|Fails by being the ultimate **distraction** and **fleeing** from Nihilism. It is a sign of "weakness and sickness" and a **patchwork** over festering sores (Vivekananda).|
