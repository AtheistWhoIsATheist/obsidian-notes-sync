## How to Use HARPA AI ChatGPT Prompts

To use page-aware ChatGPT prompts, you need a free ChatGPT account and a HARPA.AI Chrome Extension. Here are the simple 3 steps to get started:

Setting up a ChatGPT account: Navigate to the ChatGPT website and sign up for a free account. Installing the HARPA.AI Chrome Extension: Install the free HARPA.AI Chrome Plugin from the Chrome Web Store in a few clicks. Using HARPA AI ChatGPT commands: When HARPA is installed, navigate to any website and press Alt+A on your keyboard or click the plugin icon in the Chrome Toolbar. HARPA will open. Type slash symbol / in HARPA chat to bring a list of prompts and commands.

## Page-Aware Prompts

HARPA AI prompts are page-aware. HARPA AI parses the content of a web page before sending it to ChatGPT. This ensures that the information received by ChatGPT is accurate and organized, leading to more effective results.

HARPA AI is designed to be simple and straightforward, allowing anyone to use it without a technical expertise.
