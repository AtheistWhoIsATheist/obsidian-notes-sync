#### Purification Sub-Loop Execution

**Target:** Failed Concepts (Heisman's Rational Self-Destruction, Nietzsche's Will to Nothingness, Russell's Free Man's Worship).

**Micro-Pass 1: Apophatic Reduction (Stripping the Will)**

- **Heisman:** The act of **"willing death"** is the final, most desperate act of the **ego** to assert control over its own narrative. The true Nihiltheistic act is the **surrender of the will** to the Nothingness, not its final, self-glorifying exertion. The goal is not self-destruction, but **self-annihilation** (Molinos), which is passive reception.
- **Nietzsche:** The **Will to Nothingness** is still a **Will**. The NT Criterion demands the cessation of all willing, including the will to cease. The **Amor Fati** must be stripped of its heroic, self-affirming posture to become pure **acceptance** of the given horror, which is the beginning of grace.
- **Russell:** The **"ideals"** are human-made **idols** placed in the Void. This is a **"meaning-fabrication"** that avoids the true terror of the Void's indifference. The **Free Man's Worship** is a form of **pride** (Socrates' critique of the unpurified mind) that refuses to acknowledge the Absolute's sovereignty.

**Micro-Pass 2: Re-Contextualization (Grace vs. Effort)**

- **Heisman/Nietzsche:** The failure lies in positing the transition as an act of **human effort** rather than **divine grace**. **Luther** 's thunderbolt of predestination proves that salvation is "utterly beyond his own powers." The Nihiltheistic transition is not a triumph of the human will, but its **collapse** before the overwhelming power of the Absolute.
- **Russell:** The **"unyielding despair"** is a correct precondition, but the subsequent **"worship"** is misdirected. The despair must lead to the realization that the only hope lies in the **"unconditional"** (Tillich), which cannot be manufactured by human ideals.

**Micro-Pass 3: Re-Evaluation against NT Criterion**

- **Heisman/Nietzsche:****FAIL $rightarrow$ PASS (Conditional).** The concepts are valid only as descriptions of the **final, desperate struggle of the ego** before surrender. They mark the boundary of the humanly possible before the intervention of the **Grace of Nothingness**.
- **Russell:****FAIL $rightarrow$ PASS (Conditional).** The concept is valid only as a description of **secular idolatry** —the highest form of evasion for the modern, rational mind.
