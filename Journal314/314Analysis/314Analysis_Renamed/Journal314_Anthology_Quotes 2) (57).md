### Counter-Naturalism: why the brain sciences can map the Absurd yet never neutralise it

Naturalism has upgraded its explanatory arsenal—predictive-processing, default-mode network (DMN) analytics, entropic-brain statistics, REBUS-style model-relaxation—but the original wound named by Ecclesiastes’ *“striving after wind”* still bleeds. Below, we follow each flagship neuroscientific account to its limit and show how, at the event-horizon, phenomenological Absurdity **outpaces** the map.

---

#### 1 | Predictive-Processing: the brain as uncertainty-minimiser

The standard model frames consciousness as hierarchical guess-work: perception = Bayesian inference racing to cancel error. Recent overviews claim that *too much* certainty (low entropy) precipitates depression, while deliberate flirtation with chaos—art, horror, meditation—re-tunes the system. [^1] [^2]

*Counter-move.* If the mind is a “prediction machine,” the Absurd Perspective appears whenever the machine **discovers its own futility**: no prediction can lock the cosmic roulette wheel. This is not a *bug* the theory patches; it is the theory’s **boundary condition** —the point where explanatory recursion collapses into self-reference, just as Lev Shestov insists that “reason is the pathos of necessity.” The algorithm can say *why* uncertainty hurts, but *that hurt* already exceeds the algorithm because it drags value-judgement (suffering, concern) into a framework declared value-blind.

DMN research locates narrative identity in midline cortices; existential rumination spikes DMN-insula coupling, producing dread. A fresh meta-analysis shows the DMN’s evolutionary deep-time roots across species, suggesting “self-reflection” is a conserved function. [^3]

*Counter-move.* If self-storytelling turns out to be a primate loop, Augustine’s “restless heart” and Cioran’s “impossibility of living, impossibility of dying” are demoted—from metaphysical pronouncements to glitchy firmware. Naturalism here duplicates the Absurd it hoped to dissolve: “Your self is an illusion”—an *illusion* that must shoulder the full weight of knowing it is an illusion.
