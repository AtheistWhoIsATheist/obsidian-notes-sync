- Here, thou shouldest know that in every man are two kinds of men, the outer and the inner man. The soul of the spiritual man whom God moves to love Him with all his powers concentrates all its forces on the inner man. Therefore He saith, "Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart."
