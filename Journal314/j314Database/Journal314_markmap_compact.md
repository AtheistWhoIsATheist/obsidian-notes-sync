# JOURNAL314 — Compact Concept Map (Theme → Top Figures → Quotes)

## Self-Abnegation/Asceticism
### Jesus
- “Sell that ye have, and give alms…”
- “If any man will come after me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross…”
- “No man can serve two masters… ye cannot serve God and mammon.”

### Miguel de Molinos
- “All good consists in being silent, suffering, and patience…”
- “The soul’s progress is the knowledge of its own nothingness.”
- “Cease multiplicity—learn internal and mystical silence.”

### Augustine
- “I was rent by two wills… habit contending with a new love.”
- “The unchangeable is better than the changeable.”
- “Within, in the interiority where Thou art nearer to me than I am to myself.”

### Desert/Mystic Line
- On relinquishment, fasting, solitude as necessary subtraction for seeing.

### Taoist/Buddhist Strand
- On non-grasping and the useless tree; on emptiness and non-attachment.

## Detachment/Renunciation
### Jesus
- “Lay not up for yourselves treasures upon earth…”
- “Seek ye first the kingdom of God…”
- “Take no thought for the morrow…”

### Augustine
- On relinquishing habitual loves to turn toward the unchangeable light.

### Monastic/Ascetic Voices
- On poverty, stability, enclosure, and the purity of heart.

### Eckhart/Pseudo-Dionysius (Apophatic Tie-in)
- Negation as ladder; letting-go as the only path to the One.

### Kierkegaard/Heidegger (Comparative)
- Interior “dying” to immediacy; anxiety as disclosure of groundlessness.

## Temporality/Impermanence
### Jesus
- “Sufficient unto the day is the evil thereof.”
- “Consider the lilies…”
- The anti-projection of anxious planning.

### Augustine
- Memory, attention, expectation—time distends the soul; the eternal gathers it.

### Heidegger/Zapffe (Comparative)
- Care and over-consciousness of death; burden management and refusal.

## Divided Self/Inner Conflict
### Augustine
- “I was rent by two wills…”
- Habit vs. illumination; the decisive interior “yes.”

### Kierkegaard
- Despair as sickness unto death—self’s relation to itself before God.

### Modern Pessimist Line (Ligotti/Tønnessen)
- Consciousness as torment; the self as unasked-for project.

## Apophatic/Silence
### Molinos
- Internal and mystical silence; suffering as instruction; patience.
### Eckhart/Pseudo-Dionysius
- Saying by unsaying; God beyond being; the cloud of unknowing.
### Christian Hesychasm / Desert Tradition
- Quiet, breath, single-word prayer; withdrawal from multiplicity.

## Existential Dread & Anxiety (as Portal)
### Augustine / Kierkegaard / Heidegger
- Dread is not pathology but aperture; exposure precedes any truthful life-form.
### Jesus
- “Be not anxious…” not as platitude, but as consequence of renunciation.

## Quest for the Boundless/Transcendent
### Jesus
- Kingdom-priority as inversion of value; re-valuation beyond mammon.
### Augustine
- The unchangeable light and recollection.
### Mystical Line
- Union named by absence; the silence where Presence happens.
