## 3) Seven Nihilistic Thematic Axes (Axial Ontologies)
These axes normalize diverse vocabularies into a shared coordinate system (0–3 weighting: 0 none · 1 weak · 2 strong · 3 dominant).

- **OV — Ontological Void:** Direct confrontation with nothingness (abyss, emptiness, kenosis, no‑thing).  
- **EN — Ego Negation:** Self-loss, anattā, ego‑death, humility, kenotic descent.  
- **SD — Suffering & Discipline:** Affliction, asceticism, spiritual trial as epistemic catalyst.  
- **TD — Time & Death (Finite/Infinite Gap):** Being‑toward‑death, impermanence, eternity pressure.  
- **LI — Language & Ineffability:** Apophasis, paradox, limits of discourse, koan/aphorism.  
- **TP — Transcendent Presence–Absence:** God above God, void‑as‑Presence, negative theophany.  
- **EP — Ethical Praxis / Non‑Movement:** Compassion, non‑resistance, wu‑wei, contemplative praxis.

> **Interpretive Rule:** We **do not** harmonize; we **juxtapose** incompatible claims and test for *structural* convergence (pattern‑level coherence despite doctrinal antagonism).

## 4) Methodology & Phases

#### Phase I — Foundational Cartography* 
- Define terms; align vocabularies; collect canonical passages; build initial thinker profiles; draft a controlled taxonomy for each axis.

#### Phase II — Source Verification & Corpus Expansion  
- Verify editions/translations; expand corpus (primary + credible secondary); version control notes; record bibliographic metadata.

#### Phase III — Quantitative Motif Mining  
- Extract motif tokens (e.g., “night,” “desert,” “silence,” “death”); compute co‑occurrence; sketch preliminary resonance graphs; assign 0–3 axis weights per thinker per text.

#### Phase IV — Void Convergence Mapping (VCM) 
- Build cross‑tradition adjacencies: if two texts share ≥k axial signatures with similar phenomenological sequencing, mark a VCM edge; annotate with *tension tags* (e.g., “atheist–mystic collision”).

#### Phase V — Thematic Fusion Layer (TFL)  
- Collapse local clusters into **macro‑motifs** (e.g., “Kenotic Abyss,” “Suffering‑as‑Epistemology,” “Silence Grammar”); maintain doctrinal conflict flags.

#### Phase VI — Inference Layer (Abductive + Bayesian + Paraconsistent) 
- Abductive best‑explanation argument for the universality claim; Bayesian priors update with motif frequency & cross‑culture dispersion; use paraconsistent logic to retain productive contradictions without explosion.

#### Phase VII — Synthesis: _Codex Aureum_  
- Narrative synthesis + matrices + methodology appendix; archive datasets; produce teaching diagrams and practitioner notes (contemplative protocols, ethical implications).

## 9) Inference Strategy: “Temporal Trace of the Transcendent”
- **Observation:** Across cultures/epochs, void‑encounter sequences and outcomes recur with statistically significant overlaps in triggers, moods, grammars of negation, and ethical pivots.  
- **Abductive Hypothesis:** The best explanation is that human cognition/affect interfaces with a real trans‑conceptual structure (“Transcendent”) that manifests *temporally* as recurrent void‑patterns.  
- **Bayesian Update:** Prior for universality rises with dispersion across independent traditions + motif density + predictive success on out‑of‑sample texts.  
- **Paraconsistent Guardrail:** Conflicting doctrinal content does not explode inference; we privilege **structural** invariants over **semantic** unanimity.
