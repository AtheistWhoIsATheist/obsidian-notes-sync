### **Nihiltheism**: A Rigorous Definition and Conceptual Clarification

**Nihiltheism** is a philosophical methodology and experiential stance that emerges from the deliberate, direct encounter with existential nothingness, radically transcending both classical nihilism's passive resignation and existentialism's subjective affirmation. At its core, Nihiltheism is neither an affirmation nor a negation of divinity or meaning, but an active mode of phenomenological engagement in which the absolute absence of inherent meaning paradoxically reveals an ineffable presence—one that does not affirm traditional theism nor align itself with conventional atheism. Instead, Nihiltheism asserts that the stark realization of existential groundlessness can catalyze a radical experiential transformation characterized by profound ego dissolution, direct confrontation with infinite emptiness, and openness to a realm of transcendental potentiality devoid of metaphysical justification.
