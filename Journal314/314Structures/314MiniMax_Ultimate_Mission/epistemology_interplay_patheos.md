    *   **Apophatic without Kataphatic:** This can lead to nihilism, the denial of all truth. It would deny the value of kataphatic theology, and any attempt to talk about God is equally as false as calling a dog a cat.
*   **How They Work Together:**
    *   Apophatic theology helps to preserve and protect the absolute truth, allowing for a more complete and fuller understanding of God.
    *   Apophatic theology is a tool, the point of which is to remove the clutter that gets in the way of our *experience* of God and to preserve and protect God's ability to interact with us.
    *   Kataphatic theology helps us reflect on our experiences with God, which will go hand-in-hand with our apprehensions and conventions, making sure we never treat conventions as literal.
