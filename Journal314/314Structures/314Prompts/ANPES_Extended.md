*Scaling Principles*:

- **4-6**: Moderate challenge to conventional assumptions
- **7-8**: Systematic undermining of epistemic foundations

The highest scores indicate questions that generate **epistemic events** moments when consciousness encounters the limits of its own cognitive apparatus.

*Scaling Principles*:

- **4-6**: Moderate challenge to conventional morality
- **7-8**: Systematic value-system destabilization
- **9-10**: Complete axiological reconstruction from groundless ground

The highest scores indicate questions that force consciousness to become **ethically creative** rather than merely applying pre-existing moral principles.

**Transcendent Resonance Potential (TRP): The Capacity for Void-Touching**
