## 9) Diagnostic Rubrics (ready-to-run)

**A. Negation-Index (NI) — 0–1**  
Rates how much a passage relies on **subtractive method** (apophasis, renunciation, silence) to secure truth.

- Factors: explicit negation, refusal of conceptual capture, ethic of subtraction, anti-idolatry.
    
- Use: high NI → prefer via negativa synthesis; low NI → prefer constructive semantics.
    

**B. Suffering-Teleology Parser (STP)**  
Classifies the _telos_ of suffering:

- **Sanctifying:** pain → transformation (union, humility).
    
- **Clarifying:** pain → lucidity (absurd revolt, courage).
    
- **Deconstructive:** pain → collapse of speech (silence/ineffability).
    
- Use: align practices accordingly (ascetic discipline / existential fidelity / apophatic quiet).
    

**C. World-Load Audit (WLA)**  
Stress-tests pursuits (wealth, status, intellectual mastery) for **load-bearing failure** under ultimate claims.

- Output: penultimate good (keep), ultimate claim (demote), idolatrous (scrap).
    

**D. Apophatic Compression Ratio (ACR)**  
How much propositional mass is intentionally discarded to avoid falsifying the Real.

- High ACR → silence/negative predication; Low ACR → positive theorem building.
    
- Use: select language strategy that fits object of discourse.

## 12) Worked Example (compact but dense)

**Prompt:** “Place Augustine’s valuation collapse, a Buddhist anattā insight, and a Camusian absurd shock on the Finite/Infinite field; compute NI/STP/ACR; recommend a path.”

**Placement:**

- **Augustine:** Finite ↓, Infinite ↑ (revaluation). NI=0.6 (subtractive pivot), STP=Sanctifying, ACR=0.4 (keeps positive predicates).
    
- **Anattā:** Finite ↓↓↓, Infinite ↗ (nondual horizon). NI=0.8, STP=Deconstructive→Liberative, ACR=0.9.
    
- **Camus (absurd):** Finite ↓, Infinite ~0 (no metaphysical rescue). NI=0.3, STP=Clarifying, ACR=0.2.
    

**Recommendation:**

- For Augustine: contemplative re-orientation; critique of penultimates (WLA).
    
- For anattā: high-discipline apophasis; ethical non-clinging practices.
    
- For Camus: protocols of lucid fidelity (revolt, courage), avoid false transcendence.
    

**Reasoning Summary:** three paths, one seam (void). Different **vectors** explain divergent yet interoperable disciplines.
