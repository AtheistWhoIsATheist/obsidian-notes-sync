# Research Plan: Nihilistic Recurrence as Ontological Foundation

## Objective
Establish nihilistic recurrence as an ontological condition of human existence, not pathological aberration, through philosophical analysis of Journal314 data and cross-tradition synthesis.

## Research Questions
1. How does the Journal314 corpus demonstrate that confrontation with meaninglessness is constitutive of consciousness?
2. Which thinkers across traditions frame nihilistic experience as essential to authentic existence?
3. How do existential anxiety, despair, and nothingness function as fundamental to self-awareness?
4. What evidence exists from mystical, philosophical, and psychological traditions supporting this thesis?
